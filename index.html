Ting Zhang

I am currently an Associate Professor at the School of Artificial Intelligence, Beijing Normal University. I obtained my B.S. (2012) and Ph.D. (2017) from University of Science and Technology of China (USTC). I had the internship at MSRA during 2013-2017 and achieved Microsoft Research Asia Fellowship award in 2015. During my PhD, I worked on approximate nearest neighbor search. After that, I joined MSRA in 2017 and then joined Beijing Normal University in 2024. My work is mainly focused on computer vision, such as content generation, representation learning, large scale indexing. If you are interested in collaborating, discussing research ideas, or learning more about my work, please feel free to contact me at hellozting@outlook.com. [Google Scholar] [中文主页]
